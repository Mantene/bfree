The bFree Application

© Copyright 2006 The University of North Carolina.

bFree is a standalone, cross-platform application that will extract the content from a Blackboard™ course archive or export "zip" file, and create (at a designated location) either a set of folders and data or a standalone web site, in either case containing a representation of the original Blackboard™ course and its content.

Blackboard™ course authors use bFree to retrieve their course content in simple and standard formats.
